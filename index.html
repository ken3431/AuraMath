AURAMATH

PRIVACY PROTOCOL

1. OVERVIEW

AuraMath is committed to protecting user privacy while providing high-fidelity computational intelligence. This policy outlines how we handle data within the application environment.

2. DATA COLLECTION

We do not collect personal identifiable information (PII) such as your name, address, or phone number unless voluntarily submitted to the Global Leaderboard. The following data points are processed:

  • Performance Metrics: IQ scores and math results (stored locally or on D1 Database if submitted).
  • Device Identifiers: Used by Google AdMob for personalized advertising.
  • AI Queries: Mathematical strings sent to Cloudflare Edge AI for step-by-step synthesis.

3. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Our system integrates specific third-party providers to enhance functionality:

  • Google AdMob: For monetization and ad delivery.
  • Cloudflare AI: For processing complex mathematical solutions.
  • D1 Database: For hosting global rankings.

4. DATA SECURITY

All transmissions between the AuraMath client and the Cloudflare Edge nodes are encrypted. We implement SHA-256 HMAC signatures to verify the integrity of all data moving to the Global Leaderboard.

5. USER RIGHTS

Users may clear their local performance data by clearing the application cache in Android settings. For removal of scores from the Global Leaderboard, users may contact system support.

6. CONTACT

For inquiries regarding this privacy protocol, contact the system administrators via the support channels provided on the Play Store.
